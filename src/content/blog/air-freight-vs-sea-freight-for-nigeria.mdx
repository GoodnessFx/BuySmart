---
title: "Air Freight vs Sea Freight for Nigeria"
category: "Shipping Updates"
date: "July 2026"
readTime: "4 min read"
excerpt: "When speed matters, air usually wins. When cargo size and cost control matter more, sea is often the better decision."
---

Air freight works best for urgent, valuable, or lightweight products. It helps businesses restock faster and can protect margin when the product turns quickly after arrival.

Sea freight is usually better for bulky and heavy cargo where the lower shipping cost matters more than speed. It suits buyers who can plan ahead and wait longer for arrival.

The right choice depends on your product weight, package size, urgency, and selling timeline. A good shipping plan should protect both delivery speed and landed cost.
