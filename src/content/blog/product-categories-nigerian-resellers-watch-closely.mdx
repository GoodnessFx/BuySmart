category: "Market Trends"

excerpt: "A quick look at product categories that continue to attract attention from resellers and everyday buyers."
---

Hair products remain strong because they serve personal users, salon owners, and beauty resellers. Buyers in this category usually care about consistency, not just the cheapest possible price.

Phones, accessories, and smaller electronics continue to move because demand is steady and restock cycles can be faster. Supplier verification and authenticity checks are especially important here.

Home goods, appliances, and baby products also attract careful buyers who want reliable quality. These categories benefit from inspection because product finish, packaging, and material quality matter a lot after delivery.
