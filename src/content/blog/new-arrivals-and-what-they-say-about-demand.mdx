---

New arrivals are not only about fresh stock. They also show what customers are buying repeatedly and which categories are strong enough to justify another sourcing cycle.

Electronics, baby products, and mixed retail goods often reflect steady demand because they serve both personal buyers and resellers. Categories that move consistently are usually easier to restock with confidence.

The best time to plan the next order is before stock pressure becomes urgent. That gives buyers more control over supplier choice, inspection timing, and shipping cost.
