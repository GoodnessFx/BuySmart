---

Importation classes should go beyond motivational talk. Buyers need practical guidance on supplier verification, quotation review, inspection planning, and how to compare air and sea freight correctly.

Weight calculation is one of the most important topics because it affects the final shipping bill. Customers should understand actual weight, volumetric weight, and why the higher figure is usually billed.

A useful class also explains what to ask before payment, how to reduce scam risk, and how to document product specifications so the correct item is sourced from the start.
