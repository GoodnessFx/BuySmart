Build a premium, fully responsive website for **BuySmart Procurement Limited** (BUYSMART.NG), a China/Vietnam sourcing and shipping procurement company based in Lagos, Nigeria.

## Brand Voice
Confident, no-fluff, trustworthy, built for Nigerian business owners and bulk buyers. Nothing generic, nothing that sounds like stock AI copy. Short punchy sentences. No em dashes. No emojis anywhere in copy or UI — use custom SVG line icons instead (shipping box, globe, checklist, cart, container ship, QR/verify mark).

## Color System — reject default AI palettes
Do NOT use purple-to-blue gradients, generic "tech blue," or violet accents (the default AI-generated look). Instead:
- Base: deep charcoal/black (#0D0D0D – #161616)
- Accent: warm gold/brass (#C9A227 – #D4AF37), matching the brand's existing gold ring/line motif from their flyer
- Neutral: warm off-white (#F7F5F0) for light sections
- Use gold as a thin structural accent (1px rules, icon strokes, underlines) — not as large flooded backgrounds
- No dashed borders anywhere. Solid 1px hairlines only, low-opacity white or gold.

## Typography
- Headings: a strong geometric sans (e.g. Sora, General Sans, or Satoshi) — bold, tight tracking
- Body: a clean humanist sans (e.g. Inter or Manrope) for readability
- No default system font look, no Times/serif unless used deliberately for a pull-quote

## Hero Section (5 images required)
Full-width hero with a **five-image collage/grid**, not a single stock banner:
1. Large primary image: real-feeling photo of a customer receiving a package at their door (matches the flyer's brand image — confident woman holding boxes, courier at door)
2. Secondary image: a shipping container / cargo ship at port
3. Third image: warehouse or packages being processed/verified
4. Fourth image: a small business owner unboxing/reselling product (market or shop setting)
5. Fifth image: a close-up of a phone showing an order/quote request (represents "send us your item link")

Layout: one large image left or center, four smaller images arranged in a grid or offset column around it, all in a consistent gold-ringed circular or rounded-square frame to match the existing brand mark. Headline over or beside the collage:

**Headline:** "We Ship. You Sell. No Wahala."
**Subheadline:** "Factory-price sourcing and shipping from China and Vietnam — verified, calculated, delivered."
**CTA buttons:** "Request a Quote" (primary, gold fill) / "How It Works" (secondary, outline)

Use real, licensable stock photography (Unsplash/Pexels style — authentic, unposed, natural lighting) for every image on the site. No illustration-style AI art, no 3D render clichés, no glowing abstract shapes.

## Technical Requirements
- Fully mobile responsive: hero collage restacks cleanly on mobile (no cropped/broken images), stepper goes vertical, nav collapses to a clean hamburger menu (not a hamburger with 3 dashed lines — use solid bars)
- Fast-loading, compressed real images, proper alt text for every image (SEO + accessibility)
- No lorem ipsum, no placeholder gray boxes — every image slot filled with a real, relevant photo
- No AI-slop giveaways: no fake glowing gradients, no random abstract blobs, no stock "diverse team high-fiving" images, no oversized rounded-everything cards, no purple/violet anywhere
- Consistent icon set throughout (one icon library, one stroke weight, gold or white strokes only)
- Clean spacing system (consistent padding/margins, not cramped, not excessive whitespace)
- Sticky header with logo + phone number + WhatsApp CTA always visible on scroll
- Smooth but subtle scroll animations only (no flashy AI-template parallax overload)

## Deliverable Goal
The site should feel like a real, established Lagos logistics/procurement company — premium, trustworthy, built for business owners who need to move product fast. Every section must justify its images; nothing generic, nothing that screams "made with AI in 10 minutes."
